## ğŸ”§ For Admins

### Managing Projects

Projects are managed through Google Forms and Google Sheets:

1. Students/faculty submit proposals via the form
2. Review submissions in your Google Sheet
3. Add **"Approved"** in the Status column to publish projects
4. Projects automatically appear on the board (no redeployment needed!)

### Google Sheet Structure

Required columns:
- **Timestamp** - Auto-generated by form
- **Name** - Project name
- **Affiliation** - Campus affiliation
- **Please describe your project** - Project description
- **Please indicate the lab or student org...** - Organization/lab name
- **Status** - Manual approval column (type "Approved" to publish)

### Configuration

The app fetches from a public Google Sheets CSV endpoint. To update:

1. Open `src/App.jsx`
2. Update `SHEET_CSV_URL` constant with your sheet's public CSV URL
3. Rebuild and redeploy
